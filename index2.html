How to use your new Fuzmo Account

Welcome to the Fuzmo community! We are very excited to have you join us and wanted to make sure you get the most out of your new account, so please see some tips and tricks below.
Firstly, to update your profile picture & bio you can click the Settings cog on your profile page
Now find new friends! On the Explore page you can see a variety of popular pictures, latest pictures, popular pets and users, just click on them and start Following!
Double tap a picture to like a post
You can favourite hash tags by clicking the star button next to a tag on the Explore page
To view your list of favourite hash tags, you can swipe to the left of your screen on the home page and there they are!
To Tag a pet in a picture, you must first add the pet to your profile.
When uploading a picture, you can now tap on your pet to tag them in a picture
Edit a pet? Sure! On your profile open the Pets tab, click into the pet you wish to edit (or delete), and now click the Edit button
